Sydney Reservoir Burn

This map was produced in one day in collaboration with Josh Seibel as part of the UW Cartography Lab Design Challenge 2020. The theme of the design challenge was to 'Spend the day in the Newsroom' with the expert guest cartographer and graphics reporter Lauren Tierney of the Washington Post. This map was produced to accompany a fictional article discussing the impact of the 2019-2020 wildfires on the water supply for Sydney, Australia.
